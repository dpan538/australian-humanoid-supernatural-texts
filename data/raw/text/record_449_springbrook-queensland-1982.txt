Public AYR report page for Springbrook, Queensland 1982. Source-stated location: Springbrook, Queensland. Event field: Yowie Sighting. Body excerpt retained for review: Miss. Smith and her boyfriend sighted a Yowie in the forest at Springbook's "Best of all lookout" at dusk, while holidaying from Sydney. They described a noise crashing through the undergrowth of the bush, and thought at first it may have been a Wallaby until they actually saw it.
