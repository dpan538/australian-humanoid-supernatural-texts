Public AYR report page for Mt. Kembla, New South Wales 2013. Source-stated location: Mt. Kembla, New South Wales. Event field: Possible Infrasound. Body excerpt retained for review: I was bush walking with my son right at the top of Mt Kembla and we came across a deer carcass. The back leg was completely busted backwards like it had been picked up and swung around. It was torn open and everything internal removed, yet not a drop of blood. I’ve worked in the meat industry for many years and I’ve never seen anything like it.
