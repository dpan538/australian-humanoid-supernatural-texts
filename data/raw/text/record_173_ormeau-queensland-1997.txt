Body excerpt retained for review: I was standing in a field talking on my phone at 11pm in mid July 97' when I heard what appeared to be 3 people crashing through the bush behind me. I had no reason to think much about it at the time, until I became aware that the crashing turned into a quiet and slow stalking process.
