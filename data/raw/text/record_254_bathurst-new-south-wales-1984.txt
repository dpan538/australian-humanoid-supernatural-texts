Public AYR report page for Bathurst, New South Wales 1984. Source-stated location: Bathurst New South Wales. Event field: Footprint Find. Body excerpt retained for review: In Bathurst, 1984 or 85, I was sure I saw a Yowie footprint next to the river. I wrote it down in a diary but haven't dwelled on it till now. I was not looking for Yowies at the time. I was visiting a main area where families go to swim on the outskirts of Bathurst and I crossed the other side of the river downstream which went into a bush area.
