Public AYR report page for Aberfeldy, Victoria 1996. Source-stated location: Aberfeldy (near Moe), Victoria. Event field: Sighting. Body excerpt retained for review: It was about 9 or 10 pm on a School Camp at Aberfeldy about 2 hours drive from Moe at the start of the Great Dividing Range. It was a heavily bushed area and there were 20 students plus teachers on the camp.
