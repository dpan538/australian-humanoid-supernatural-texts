Body excerpt retained for review: We left Harvey Bay at about 6 O’clock in the morning, through Maryborough and down the Woolooga Road towards Kilkivan. Between Wide Bay Road and Woolooga, the creature was standing on the left hand side of the road in scrubby bushland. It was fenced further on into a farm, but the side of the road was scrubby bush about 1 metre high with a few tall trees.
