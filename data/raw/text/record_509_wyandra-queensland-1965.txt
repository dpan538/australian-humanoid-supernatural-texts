Public AYR report page for Wyandra, Queensland 1965. Source-stated location: Wyandra, Queensland. Event field: Yowie Roadside Sighting. Body excerpt retained for review: A local mailman saw a 6ft Yowie swagger across the road 200m ahead of him, and described it as a "hairy man". The local Policeman of the time, Snr. Con. Binigham stood up for the man’s integrity saying he was an honest and reliable man.
