Body excerpt retained for review: Back when Worongary was called Alkira Park, as kids we used to wander the streets, roller skate and walk through the long grass, and we would visit the creeks etc. A girlfriend of mine lived in a new estate and that would often have very long grass. We used to walk each other halfway home, and then run back to our own homes.
