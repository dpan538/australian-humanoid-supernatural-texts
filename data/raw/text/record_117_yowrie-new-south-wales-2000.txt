Body excerpt retained for review: Hi Dean, the time of day would have been mid afternoon, sunny, clear skies around Feb. or March. The year was about 2000. I was returning from a bush walk and had just got back onto a wombat trail I had started my walk from, I was looking at the ground (the trail)and as I lifted my gaze to peer along the trail, coming towards me was the yowie.
