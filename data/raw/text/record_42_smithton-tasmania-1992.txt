Public AYR report page for Smithton, Tasmania 1992. Source-stated location: Smithton, Tasmania. Event field: Possible Yowie Sighting. Body excerpt retained for review: This was around 1992. We were out shooting and we saw a set of eyes. We sat there looking at them for a good 20 minutes, this was 3 or 4 of us, and as soon as we started moving towards us it just bolted off. When we got over there, I was standing on the back of the truck and I ended up being eye height with it.
