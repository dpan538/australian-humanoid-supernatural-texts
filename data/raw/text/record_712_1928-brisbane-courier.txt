Body excerpt retained for review: SOON after I first visited the Auburn River I made the acquaintance of Chapman, then king by heredity of the Hawkwood tribe or rather what remained of it. Chapman had a grievance, and he explained it fully to me as we sat in the shade of a large gumtree. The quarter moon plate and chain showing kingship had never been issued to him, by the Government.
