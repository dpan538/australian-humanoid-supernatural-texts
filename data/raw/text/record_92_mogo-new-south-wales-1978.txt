Public AYR report page for Mogo, New South Wales 1978. Source-stated location: Mogo, New South Wales. Event field: Yowie Sighting. Body excerpt retained for review: I was probably around 8 or 9 years old and went to the Mogo School. Around the School was all State Forest. We all used to go up into the forest and sit there on chairs, have small fires etc. It was quite a community because we all went to School together.
