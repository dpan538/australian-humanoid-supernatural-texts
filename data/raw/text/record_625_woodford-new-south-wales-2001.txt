Body excerpt retained for review: I leave home at about 4:15 am to catch the train to work, and this particular morning, I was walking up the driveway, when I saw this huge dark figure on the footpath looking down at me. I was at eye level with it, but it would have been about 50 metres away from me. I would say it was about 7 feet tall.
