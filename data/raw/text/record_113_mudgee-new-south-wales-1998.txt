Public AYR report page for Mudgee, New South Wales 1998. Source-stated location: Mudgee, New South Wales. Event field: Audible/Smell. Body excerpt retained for review: About ten years ago I was invited to take part on a recreation of an early settler type hunting trip at a secluded location near Mudgee NSW. Using nothing but correct period clothing, camping gear and muskets, the object was to ascertain the possible failure or success of early period hunters in an unfamiliar land.
