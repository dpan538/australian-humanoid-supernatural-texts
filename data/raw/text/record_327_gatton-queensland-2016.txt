Body excerpt retained for review: In January 2016 I worked as a fruit picker in Gatton QLD. At the 10th of January I waited for my pickup to work in front of our flat, overlooking the street, facing the woods on the opposite side of the street. I must have been 4.30 - 5.00 am, the weather was dry and clear and the sun was just coming up.
