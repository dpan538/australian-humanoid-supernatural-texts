Body excerpt retained for review: It was about 9 O’Clock’ish at night or later and we (myself and my partner), were making our way up the windy roads into the hills driving our Subaru Forester, and something crossed the road ahead. I said to my partner, ‘What the heck was that’. She seemed a little freaked out. She has a University degree in Science, with Honours, an educated type.
