Public AYR report page for Trentham, Victoria 2015. Source-stated location: Trentham, Victoria (Domino Trail). Event field: Yowie Sighting. Body excerpt retained for review: Saw a tall dark figure walk across the Domino Trail about 3.5km south-west of Trentham heading towards Lyonville. It came up an embankment from the bush from the left, stood briefly in the middle of the trail while appearing look at me and my dog, then walked into the bush down the opposite embankment.
