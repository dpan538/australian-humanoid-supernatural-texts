Public AYR report page for Dungay Creek, New South Wales 1962. Source-stated location: Dungay Creek, New South Wales. Event field: Yowie Sighting. Body excerpt retained for review: I was about 10 or 11 years old at the time. My sister and two brothers went from the house, which was in the bush, down to pick some oranges which was about 2kms away. We had one of those trolley carts dragging behind us, and the dog started going mad at something in the bush. We just thought it was going mad over a rabbit or something.
