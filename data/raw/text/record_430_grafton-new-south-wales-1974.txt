Body excerpt retained for review: A Grafton man is set to join the latest North Coast Yowie hunt, with a report of a sighting of the fabled beast near Grafton 16 years ago. Mr. Michael Allison of South Grafton and his two brothers spotted what they firmly believe was a Yowie on the Grafton-Casino road, one night in the summer of 1974.
