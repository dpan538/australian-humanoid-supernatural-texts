Public AYR report page for Mt. Kembla, New South Wales 1983. Source-stated location: Mt Kembla, New South Wales. Event field: Yowie Sighting. Terrain field: Bush clearing. I was 19 or 20, and in the mountains with my wife to be.. Body excerpt retained for review: It was about 10 to 12 at night. We were parked in the bush, in a clearing off a dirt track about 7/8ths of the way up Mount Kembla road, and had been there about 40 minutes, in the back of the car. I got this strange feeling, like a sudden dread, like the blood draining out of you.
