Body excerpt retained for review: I have been doing some research on a bizarre experience I had last night, and came across this website. Last night I was at the Mt Kembla lookout, that over looks Lake Illawarra. I had been there, sitting in my car for about 15 minutes, and heard some rather large sticks being broken in the distance, like something was walking through the forest.
