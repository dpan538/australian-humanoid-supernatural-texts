# Ethics And Sensitivity

The project uses public, published, openly discoverable sources, but public discoverability is not permission to reproduce restricted cultural knowledge.

For Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander-related material, V2 records:

- source/community terminology;
- who supplied the classification where public;
- cultural sensitivity;
- ethics review status;
- display mode;
- publicness and reuse assessment;
- correction, amendment, or takedown notes where relevant.

Do not collect restricted, secret/sacred, unpublished, or community-controlled non-public content. Do not infer secret/sacred information from public snippets. Do not convert ancestral or spirit-person narratives into cryptid species.

Display modes:

- `full`
- `summary_only`
- `metadata_only`
- `suppressed`

High-sensitivity materials default to `summary_only` or `metadata_only` until reviewed.
