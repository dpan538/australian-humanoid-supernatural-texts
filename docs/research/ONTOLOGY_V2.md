# Ontology V2

V2 separates source wording from analytical organization.

`entity_labels` preserve exact source terminology. Labels such as `Yahoo`, `Hairy Man`, `Australian gorilla`, `wild man`, `Mimih`, `Quinkan`, or source/community terminology must not be silently rewritten as `Yowie`.

`entity_concepts` are analytical organization concepts only. They are not species, biological taxa, or proof of supernatural entities.

The hairy-humanoid operational cluster may group Yowie, Yahoo, Hairy Man, Hairy Man of the Wood, Australian ape, Australian gorilla, wild man, ape-like man, and unknown humanoid for querying and review. This is an operational cluster, not an ontology of real beings.

Traditional and spirit-person materials are handled separately and conservatively. Yaroma, Yara-ma-yha-who, Mimih/Mimi, Quinkan/Quinkin, Wandjina/Wanjina, Nargun, Garkain, Mokoi, Mamu, and Pangkarlangu must not be merged into the Yowie cluster.

Wandjina/Wanjina and other culturally central public materials require high sensitivity flags, source/community terminology, ethics review, and summary-only or metadata-only display where appropriate.
